Step 1 — Computer

Install the Coach plugin

Coach uses Claude Code to analyze your food photos and generate personalized plans. Run this in your terminal to install the plugin:

curl -sL https://raw.githubusercontent.com/nEmily/health-tracker/main/install-coach.sh | bash
irm https://raw.githubusercontent.com/nEmily/health-tracker/main/install-coach.ps1 | iex
1

Run the installer

Installs the Coach skill, creates your data directory, and downloads profile templates.

2

Run the setup conversation

Open Claude Code and type /setup. Your coach will ask about your goals, body stats, and preferences, then generate your personalized plan.

3

Connect your phone

Setup generates a pairing link. Open it on your phone to connect — or use the section below.

Step 2 — Phone

Connect your phone

After running /setup, you'll get a sync key. Enter it here to generate a pairing QR code — scan it with your phone to install and connect the app.
